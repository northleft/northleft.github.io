What are these awesome drawings?

Years ago (long before the pandemic), what started as a programming challenge led to the creation of my Drawing Machine.

It has since evolved into a comprehensive set of tools that can render artwork featuring intricate, symmetrical designs consisting of looping, ribbon-like structures. I use math to form lines and meshes to create dynamic, almost fluid-like shapes.

It is an art of passion — I’ve spent many hours figuring things out and developing new tools.
