What’s with all these awesome drawings?

From time to time, I dabble as an artist.

My early career was as an artist. Unfortunately (or fortunately?), my career was short lived. I’m much better with code than with the brush, but the love and appreciation of art has always held me.

A few years ago, I found a way to combine both of my interests. A pre-covid challenge I gave myself - to make a spirograph generator. This project evolved into a set of tools I use to generate these fantastic shapes. These shapes are an expression of the beauty and simplicity of math.

This project was a perfect excuse to sharpen my knowledge of ES6 javascript and grow the tools I use to make art.
